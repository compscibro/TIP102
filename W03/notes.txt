Stack Methods

    push is O(1)
    pop is O(1)
    peek stack[-1] is O(1)

Queue Methods

    enqueue is O(1)
    dequeue is O(1)
    peek is O(1)

popleft() -> pop from left
pop() -> pop from right

Note, there's no popright. The result should be the same if there's only one element.

Each of them have their own advantages. Stack can only pop() from right. 
Deque can pop and append from either end. List is flexible with what you want to implement.
